The Rescue

In 2018, for two weeks, the world is under the spell of the rescue of the Thai football team who became trapped in a cave several kilometers deep. The rushing army, the Navy Seals and the flown in American Special Forces are powerless. No one appears to have the proper training and equipment to free the young footballers from these miles of narrow caves, where the water can flow as fast as a river and be pitch black from the mud.
