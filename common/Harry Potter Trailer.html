Harry Potter

On his eleventh birthday, Harry Potter learns that his dead parents were two powerful wizards and that he also has magical powers himself. He is educated at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. That's where the adventure of his life begins. Harry proves to be a real high flyer in Quidditch, playing an exciting chess game with live chess pieces on his way to an encounter with a very dark magician.
