MY SALINGER YEAR

New York, the 90s. After graduating, Joanna dreams of becoming a writer. She finds a job as an assistant to Margaret, an unflappable and also old-fashioned literary agent, with JD Salinger as one of her most famous clients. Joanna, meanwhile, lives in two opposing worlds. Because during the day she immerses herself in the world of glamor in the stately office full of old-fashioned dictaphones, typewriters and lunches where three martinis are the norm, at night it is poverty in the house where she lives with her socialist boyfriend, but without a sink.
