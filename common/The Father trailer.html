The Father

The Father is a fascinating portrait of a man who is losing his grip on reality. The brilliant rendition of Anthony Hopkins (The Silence of the Lambs, The Two Popes) will never let you go. Olivia Colman (The Crown, The Favourite) as his daughter Anne, makes both her powerlessness and her love tangible. Anne is once again looking for a nurse for her demented father Anthony, who keeps refusing all help.
